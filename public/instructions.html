How to Use the Library Management System

Follow this step-by-step guide to make the most of your digital library experience.

1. Creating Your Account

  1. Click "Login" in the navigation menu
  2. Select the "Sign Up" tab
  3. Enter your full name as registered with the university
  4. Use your university email address (@univen.ac.za)
  5. Create a secure password (minimum 6 characters)
  6. Click "Create Account"

Note: You'll be automatically logged in after successful registration.

2. Logging In

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Login"

Forgot your password? Contact the library administrator for assistance.

3. Guest Access

Don't have an account yet? You can still browse our catalog:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click "Guest Access" tab
  3. Click "Continue as Guest"

Note: Guests can browse books but cannot borrow them. Create an account to access full features.

4. Browsing the Library Catalog

  1. Click "Library" in the navigation menu
  2. Browse all available books in the catalog
  3. View book details including title, author, and category
  4. Check availability status (Available/Borrowed)

Tip: Books show their current availability status and number of copies available.

5. Borrowing Books

  1. Find a book you want to borrow in the catalog
  2. Ensure the book shows "Available" status
  3. Click the "Borrow Book" button
  4. Confirm your borrowing request
  5. The system automatically sets your due date (14 days from borrowing)

Important: You can only borrow books that are currently available. Each book has a 14-day borrowing period.

6. Managing Your Borrowed Books

  1. Click "My Books" in the navigation menu
  2. View all your currently borrowed books
  3. Check due dates for each book
  4. See if any books are overdue (highlighted in red)

Stay organized: Check your borrowed books regularly to avoid overdue fees.

7. Returning Books

  1. Go to "My Books" page
  2. Find the book you want to return
  3. Click "Return Book" button
  4. Confirm the return
  5. The book will be removed from your borrowed list

Note: Once returned, the book becomes available for other students to borrow.

Important Guidelines

Borrowing Rules

  • Each book can be borrowed for 14 days
  • You cannot borrow the same book twice simultaneously
  • Books must be returned by the due date to avoid penalties
  • Only registered students can borrow books

Account Security

  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Log out when using shared computers
  • Use your university email address for registration
  • Contact library staff if you suspect unauthorized access

Technical Support

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Use modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Clear your browser cache if you experience issues
  • Contact IT support for technical problems

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using the library system:

  • Visit the library help desk during operating hours
  • Email the library administration
  • Check the About page for more information about system features

Ready to Start?

Now that you know how to use the system, create your account and start exploring our digital library!
